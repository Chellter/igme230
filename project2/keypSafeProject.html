Keyp Safe, February 2018

Design Project

I designed a logo for a ficticious company by utilizing several design skills.

I started by taking pictures of a key under different lighting. Then I sketched different black and white keys based on the photos and chose the best one. I graphically translated it into Adobe Illustrator. Finally, I added text to complete my combination logo.
